# Bitwarden for Business Admins

This article is designed to introduce Bitwarden administrators to all the features and functionality available with Teams and Enterprise business plans. Follow these video guides and discover key steps for security success. Learn what is available with your plan, how to import passwords from an existing solution, share items across the organization, recover a user’s account, and other topics important to your implementation.
