# Update Your Billing Information

Your billing information can only be updated from the Bitwarden web app.

> [!NOTE] Individual and organization subscriptions are separate
> Subscriptions to individual plans and organization plans are **separate**; you will need to update your billing information in separate areas. If you have **both** a premium individual subscription and an organization subscription, updating billing information for one will not update the other. Not sure which plan you're on? For individuals, check **Settings** → **Subscription**; for organizations, owners can check **Billing** → **Subscription** in the Admin Console.

## Update billing information for individuals

To update your individual subscription's billing information, go to **Settings** → **Subscription** → **Payment details**:

![Premium payment method](https://bitwarden.com/assets/1VADjEEgyXqp4RxGTJE5pn/be179fbba8bb435accfb26c1f0c75ea3/Premium_payment_method.png)

On this page, you can:

- **Add Credit** (PayPal or Bitcoin) that can be used to make purchases. Available credit will be automatically applied towards invoices generated for this account.
- **Change Payment Method** (credit card or PayPal)
- **Add a Payment Method** (credit card or PayPal)

Select the **Billing history** tab to view and download **Invoices** and **Transactions**.

## Update billing information for organizations

> [!NOTE] Owner permission required
> You must be an [Owner](https://bitwarden.com/help/user-types-access-control/) to update billing information, billing email, or organization name for your organization.

### Update payment details and billing address

To update your organization's payment details, including its [VAT or GST number](https://bitwarden.com/help/tax-calculation/#value-added-tax-vat/) where applicable:

1. In the Bitwarden web app, open the Admin Console using the product switcher:

   ![Product switcher](https://bitwarden.com/assets/2uxBDdQa6lu0IgIEfcwMPP/e3de3361749b6496155e25edcfdcf08b/2024-12-02_11-19-56.png)

2. From the navigation, select **Billing** → **Payment details**:

   ![Organization payment details](https://bitwarden.com/assets/1ueaSUjVlMBXtjHroCB4Kw/38a6da67cb49413087787b57d62a2b2a/Organization_payment_method.png)

This screen gives you the options to:

- **Change payment method** (credit card or PayPal)
- **Edit billing address**
- **Add credit** (PayPal or Bitcoin) that can be used to make purchases. Available credit will be automatically applied towards invoices generated for this account.

> [!NOTE] Subscription still disabled after updating?
> If your billing information has been updated but your subscription remains disabled, please [contact us](https://bitwarden.com/contact/).

### Update billing email

To update the billing email for your organization:

1. In the Bitwarden web app, open the Admin Console using the product switcher.
2. From the navigation, select **Settings** → **Organization info**.
3. Update your **Billing email** and select **Save**.

### Update organization name

To update the name of your organization:

1. In the Bitwarden web app, open the Admin Console using the product switcher.
2. From the navigation, select **Settings** → **Organization info**.
3. Update your **Organization name** and select **Save**.
